How to Open an Account

1

Submit Application

Fill out the account opening form available at Office or contact from our website.

2

Provide Documents

Submit required documents: Citizenship certificate, passport-size photos, and proof of address.

3

Verification

Our staff will verify your documents and complete KYC (Know Your Customer) process.

4

Account Activation

Make initial deposit and receive your account number, passbook.
