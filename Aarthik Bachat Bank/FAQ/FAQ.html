Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our banking services, accounts, loans, and more. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

General Banking Questions

You can open an account through this method:

  • Online: Visit our website and contact us online through Whatsapp for account opening.
  • Branch: Visit any of our Office with required documents

Required Documents: Proof of identity: Citizenship Card, and 2 passport-sized photographs. The initial deposit requirement varies by account type.

Office Banking Hours:

  • Sunday to Friday: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Saturday: Closed

Customer Support: Available 10/6 at 051-419294 for emergency services.

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification requires the following documents:

Proof of Identity (any one):

  • NID Card
  • Citizenship Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License

Proof of Address (any one):

  • Utility Bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Property Tax Receipt
  • Rent Agreement with landlord's

Account Related Questions

Minimum balance requirements vary by account type:

  • Savings Account: NPR 100/-
  • Normal Savings Account: NPR 100/-
  • Current Savings Account: NPR 100/-
  • Artha Barsik Bachat Khata: NPR 100/-

Note: Interest is calculated annually and credited once per year.

Eligibility requirements vary by account type:

  • Savings Account: Any individual with valid ID
  • Normal Account: Any individual with valid ID
  • Current Account: Any business or individual
  • Artha Barsik Bachat Khata: Any individual above 18 years

Account closure rules vary by account type:

  • Savings Account: Yes, after formalities
  • Normal Account: Yes, after formalities
  • Current Account: Yes, Any time
  • Artha Barsik Bachat Khata: Yes, after formalities

Note: All pending deposits and interest will be settled before closure

Interest calculation varies by account type:

  • Savings Account: Calculated annually at 10% on closing balance
  • Normal Account: Calculated annually at 10% on closing balance
  • Current Account: No Interest
  • Artha Barsik Bachat Khata: Calculated Semi-Annually at 5% on closing balance

Note: Interest is credited once per year, and only if the minimum balance is maintained

Loans & Credit Questions

Required documents vary by loan type:

Proof of Identity (any one):

  • Citizenship / ID proof
  • Income proof / Salary slips
  • Business documents (for business loans)
  • Collateral documents (if required)
  • Voter ID Card
  • Driving License

Note: Additional documents may be requested depending on loan type.

Loan interest depends on loan type and tenure:

  • Personal Loan: Calculated EMI at 14.55% interest rate
  • Business Loan: Calculated EMI at 14.55% interest rate
  • Home Loan: Calculated EMI at 14.55% interest rate

Note: Interest rate is same on every type of Loan.

Yes, you can prepay your loan subject to these conditions:

  • Home Loans: 5 years to 25 years
  • Personal Loans: 6 months to 5 years
  • Business Loans: 1 year to 7 years

Note: Early repayment may be allowed, subject to terms and conditions.

Quick Answers to Popular Questions

What documents are required to open an account?

Copy of Citizenship Certificate or valid government-issued ID, Recent passport-size photographs, PAN number (if applicable), Nominee details (name and relationship).

How can we apply for Loan?

Loan approval is subject to eligibility criteria, document verification, and bank policies. For more details, applicants are requested to contact the Office directly.

What happens if I miss a loan installment?

Missing a loan installment may result in late fees and affect your credit history. Please contact the bank for assistance.

How long does loan approval take?

Loan approval time depends on loan type, document verification, and eligibility. Typically, it takes 3–7 working days.

How much Interest rate we get in each account?

Get 10% interest rate per annum.

Is ATM/Debit card available

No! We don't provide any ATM/Debit cards.

Can Minor allowed to open account?

Age group between 16 to 18 are allowed but below 16 are not allowed with proper verification.
