Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings in 2075 B.S to becoming a trusted financial partner for thousands.

Historical Timeline

2075

The Beginning

Aarthik Saving & Credit Co-operative was founded by 3 visionary members in Birgunj with a small capital of NPR 50,000. Our mission was simple: to provide accessible financial services to the local community.

2077

First Own Building

Expanded our reach by opening our first official building in Parsa District. Membership grew to 500 members, and assets crossed NPR 80 thousand, marking our first major growth milestone.

2078

Website Transformation

Developed our first official website, revolutionizing member services. Introduced fixed deposit schemes and loans, becoming a comprehensive financial service provider online.

2080

5th Anniversary

Celebrating five years of dedicated service, Aarthik Saving and Credit Co-operative Ltd. proudly marked its 5th anniversary, reflecting on trust, growth, and community support.

2081

Strong Financial Growth

A year of steady financial growth, where the bank showed improved performance, stronger financial stability, and continued dedication to ethical, member-focused banking.

2082

Trust by the Community

Continuing its journey of progress, the bank remains committed to community trust, sustainable growth, and delivering modern, reliable financial services.
