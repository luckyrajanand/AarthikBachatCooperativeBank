Our Purpose & Aspiration

Guided by strong principles and a clear vision, we strive to empower communities through financial inclusion and sustainable growth.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals, families, and communities by providing accessible, reliable, and innovative financial services that promote economic growth, financial literacy, and sustainable development.

We are committed to building trust through transparency, fostering financial inclusion for all segments of society, and creating value for our members through responsible banking practices.

Our mission extends beyond banking – we aim to be catalysts for positive change in the communities we serve, supporting education, entrepreneurship, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred financial cooperative in Nepal, recognized for our commitment to member satisfaction, community development, and innovative financial solutions.

We envision a financially inclusive society where every individual has access to quality banking services, opportunities for growth, and the tools to achieve financial independence.

By 2030, we aim to expand our reach to serve 100,000 members across 25 districts, while maintaining our core values of integrity, excellence, and social responsibility.

Strategic Goals 2083-2090

01

Digital Transformation

Implement comprehensive digital banking solutions to serve 90% of transactions through digital channels.

02

Financial Inclusion

Extend services to rural areas and reach 10,000 new members from underserved communities.

03

Sustainable Growth

Achieve 20% annual growth while maintaining strong asset quality and capital adequacy.

04

Community Development

Invest 5% of annual profits in community development and financial literacy programs.
